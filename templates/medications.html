Diabetes Medications

Managing diabetes involves both medications and healthy lifestyle choices. Here are some recommendations:

Medications

  • Insulin injections
  • Oral medications
  • Other prescribed medications

Healthy Habits

  • Monitor blood sugar levels regularly
  • Follow a balanced diet
  • Engage in regular physical activity
  • Stay hydrated
  • Get enough sleep

Regular Check-ups

  • Consult your healthcare provider regularly
  • Monitor cholesterol and blood pressure
  • Check kidney function
  • Eye and foot exams
